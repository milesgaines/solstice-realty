Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Solstice International Realty (“Solstice,” “we,” “us”), operated by Donna Bohana, Broker/Owner (California DRE# 01194141), respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and our lead-monitoring application, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Who we are

Solstice International Realty · Donna Bohana, Broker/Owner · (310) 801-0265 · info@solsticeir.com · California, USA.

2. Information we collect on this website

  • Information you give us: when you submit a contact, tour, or valuation request, we collect the name, email, phone number, message, and any property address you provide.
  • Property valuation inputs: addresses you enter into the home-value tool, used to generate an automated estimate.
  • Saved items: listings you favorite are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to us.
  • Usage data: standard technical information (such as pages viewed and general device/browser type) to keep the site working.

3. Our lead-monitoring application

We operate an automated application (including a Reddit “Devvit” application) that monitors publicly available posts on platforms such as Reddit and Bluesky for people publicly seeking real-estate help in the communities we serve (for example, someone publicly asking for a realtor or a home to rent in Malibu).

For matching public posts, the application processes and stores: the public post text, the public username/handle, a link to the post, the timestamp, and the community it appeared in. This information is transmitted to and stored in our backend database hosted by Supabase (enktupvwcsojqthiimvu.supabase.co) solely so we can consider responding, publicly and courteously, to offer assistance.

The application does not access private messages, non-public content, passwords, or any information you have not shared publicly. It does not post, comment, or message on your behalf automatically — any outreach is written and sent by a person.

4. How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide real-estate brokerage services.
  • To generate the automated home-value estimates you request.
  • To identify and respond to people publicly seeking real-estate assistance in our markets.
  • To operate, secure, and improve our website and application.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (for example, our database host Supabase, and property-data providers), and only as needed. Listing data is provided through the MLS/IDX and remains subject to the originating brokerage’s terms. We may disclose information if required by law.

6. Data retention

We keep inquiry information for as long as needed to serve you and to meet legal and recordkeeping obligations, then delete or anonymize it. Public-post leads are retained only while they remain relevant and are removed on request.

7. Your choices and rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information — including asking us to remove a public post captured by our application — by emailing info@solsticeir.com. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know and the right to delete; we honor those requests and will not discriminate against you for exercising them.

8. Cookies & local storage

We use minimal browser storage to remember your saved listings and preferences. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

9. Children

Our services are intended for adults and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; the “last updated” date above reflects the latest version. Material changes will be posted here.

11. Contact

Questions or privacy requests: info@solsticeir.com or (310) 801-0265.
